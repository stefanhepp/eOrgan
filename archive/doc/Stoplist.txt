o) Hammond
- Lowest Octave:
  C: Cancel Presets
  C# - A: Presets
  B flat: Use first drawbar set
  B: Use second drawbar set
- 5 Drawbar sets
  2 Drawbar sets: upper keyboard (B, B flat)
  1 Drawbar set: Pedal (6 drawbars)
  2 Drabwar sets: lower keyboard (B, B flat)
  Per set: 9 drawbars, range 0..8 (0: off, 8: full on); >8' red, octaves grey, other black
      16' (red), 5 1/3' (red), 8' (grey), 4' (grey), 2 2/3' (black), 
      2' (grey), 1 3/5' (black) , 1 1/3' (black), 1' (grey)
  Registrations: 9-digit sequence per set of drawbars
- Percussion: Only available with B (second drawbar set)
  Percussion disables 8. (black) drawbar (sound of percussion instead of 8 )
  Adds 2nd, 3rd overtones to attack envelope of notes, fades quickly
  4 Tablets, right side (front/back):
    - Percussion Off/On
    - Percussion Volume: Normal/Soft
      Normal: fades, harder/louder
      Soft: does not fade, softer
    - Percussion Delay: Slow/Fast
    - Percussion Harmonic Selector: Second/Third
      Second: Percussion one octave higher
      Third: Percussion one octave + fifth higher
- Left side:
  3 Tablets:
    - Volume: Normal/Soft
    - Vibrato Swell On/Off
    - Vibrato Great On/Off
  Vibrato/Chorus Dial: 
    V1: least amount of vibrato
    C1: least amount of chorus
    V2 (standard), C2, V3, C3
- Volume Pedal
- Leslie speaker
  - Spin on/off switch
  - Fast/Slow switch
